suv

INVENTOR: Brooks Stevens

HISTORY: 1946

A sport utility vehicle (SUV) is a car classification that combines elements of road-going passenger cars with features from off-road vehicles, such as raised ground clearance and four-wheel drive.

diesel engine characteristics

Fuel saving

Diesel engines can save 25-30% more fuel than gasoline engines.

More torque

Diesel engines can handle heavier loads because they have more torque and a better power-to-weight ratio.F

Lower emissions

Modern diesel engines produce less carbon dioxide and carbon monoxide than their counterparts.

The cost of diesel fuel has been a barrier to the widespread acceptance of diesel vehicles.

High price

A diesel car is usually more expensive to buy than a gasoline-powered counterpart.

Noise

Modern diesel engines are less noisy than before, but they are still louder than gasoline engines.

Types of automobile fuel

fuel :
Automotive fuel is the main source of energy that drives the engine and allows for the movement and power needed for driving. Automotive fuels are available in different types, and each type has a different impact on performance, economy, and the environment.

Gasoline :

It is one of the most common types of petroleum used in vehicles, bicycles and various means of transportation, and this name was given to it in the United States, and it is one of the forms that are manufactured from extracted petroleum materials and has special specifications suitable for car engines, bicycles and other vehicles, and in fact gasoline is classified based on the octane ratio available in it, which is in three recognized grades, represented by 87, 90 and 95 gasoline, which are global standards that can vary slightly from one country to another

Diesel :

It is mainly manufactured from oils used in cooking such as corn, sunflower, soybean and other vegetable oils, or from animal fats, so it is considered an environmentally friendly type of fuel, as these oils are recycled and made into a fuel that can be used in some vehicles and has proven very effective in use and can be considered a real alternative in the future to petroleum materials, both gasoline and diesel.F

Biodiesel :

It is mainly manufactured from oils used in cooking such as corn, sunflower, soybean and other vegetable oils, or from animal fats, so it is considered an environmentally friendly type of fuel, as these oils are recycled and made into a fuel that can be used in some vehicles and has proven very effective in use and can be considered a real alternative in the future to petroleum materials, both gasoline and diesel.

Ethanol :

It is one of the types of fuels that are not popular in terms of use, and is usually added to gasoline to improve engine performance. In fact, many car manufacturers have contributed to the production of a few engines that fully use ethanol in operational processes, and this type of fuel is characterized as environmentally friendly as it is manufactured from plant-based renewable sources such as sugar cane, corn and other renewable sources, so it can be considered a real alternative in the future to save cost and reduce carbon emissions resulting from the usual petroleum materials.
